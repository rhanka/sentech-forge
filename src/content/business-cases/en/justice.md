# Data & AI Roadmap - Ministry of Justice

## Budget €19M • 4 business lines

Under the Prime Minister's supervision, the Ministry of Justice defined its data and AI roadmap. It established shared governance with business departments and prioritized around thirty initiatives—from anonymizing court decisions to industrializing analytics governance and adopting API patterns for criminal, civil, and penitentiary justice.
