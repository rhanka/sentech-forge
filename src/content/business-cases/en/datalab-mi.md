Creation and management of the Ministry of Interior's data and AI innovation laboratory (2015-2019). Structuring of a multidisciplinary lab (data scientists, developers, designers) to accelerate innovation. Inventory of 80+ use cases, launch of 30+ initiatives covering Border Police, Road Safety, Immigration, Elections. The lab evolved into the Digital Factory (FabNum MI) and continues to incubate government startups (HistoVec, CartAV, Previsecours).
