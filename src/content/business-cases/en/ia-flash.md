Development of an artificial intelligence solution embedded in automatic speed cameras to detect multiple infractions simultaneously. Beyond speed control, AI cameras detect seat belt violations, phone usage while driving, and failure to maintain safe distances. National 2025-2030 program to modernize the camera network with video and real-time AI capabilities.
