Development of a mapping and analysis system for road traffic accidents. CartAV enables visualization and analysis of accidents recorded by law enforcement, including geolocation, victim characteristics, and vehicle data. Essential tool for the National Inter-ministerial Road Safety Observatory (ONISR) and prevention policies.
